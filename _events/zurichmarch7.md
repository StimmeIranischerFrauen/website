## Solidarity Rally in Zurich – Standing with the People of Iran

We, residents of Switzerland, stand in solidarity with the people of Iran and their courageous fight for freedom and in support of the Lion and Sun Revolution.

At a time when the Iranian regime has imposed a digital blackout to silence its citizens, we refuse to let their voices be erased. The people of Iran have made their will clear — they demand freedom, dignity, and democratic change.

From Switzerland, we will amplify their voices and bring their call for liberty to the Swiss public.

**Join us in raising awareness.**

**Join us in standing for freedom.**

**Join us in amplifying the voice of the Iranian people.**
